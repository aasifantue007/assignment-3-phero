The Legal Practice Areas

These are our legal practice areas wo provide services on. These are our strength and we make sure
our client's gets full support regarding these below mentioned laws.

Business Law

Business law is a body of laws that govern the formation, operation, and dissolution of businesses and organizations

Criminal Law

Criminal law is a body of laws that define and prohibit conduct deemed to be harmful to society as a whole and provide for punishment.

Child Support

Child support laws are legal guidelines that require a non-custodial parent to financially support their minor children.

Education Law

Education law encompasses the laws and regulations that govern the operation and administration.

Divorce Law

Divorce law is a branch of family law that deals with the legal dissolution of a marriage and the resulting issues.

Tax Law

Tax law is a body of laws that govern how taxes are imposed, collected and enforced by the government.
